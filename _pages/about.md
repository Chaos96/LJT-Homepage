I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.cse.ust.hk/) at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://www.ust.hk/), advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://nlp.cse.ust.hk/~junxian/).

I graduated from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/) (SJTU) in June 2024, where I received the Zhiyuan Honor Scholarship.

My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. My research interests include:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
